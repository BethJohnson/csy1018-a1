My First Set of Commissioned Work

In November 2016 I recieved my first commission which I readily accepted. It consisted of creating a new logo, and documentation for'Finnestra' a sub-company of 'Simplicity Blinds'. This was a good chance for me to practise time managment successfully as I had to manage univeristy work and this project. I eventually received a call from the owners of the company, my clients, who were impressed with my work and requested that I also take on another project. This task involved the same concept as the intial commission with one large difference...They wished for me to re-invent their main business logo/documentation. From this project I once again recieved another projec from them on top of the two previous commissions, this time for business cards for the main company.
The main issues I encountered with the project were that I had only touched upon Graphic Design as a self-taught hobby but luckily this only hindered my confidence not my clients. The other main issue I encountered was that I had never created business cards before and was not aware of the current standards for proffessional business cards. I contacted my client and obtained their previous card as well as doing some research into other layouts of business cards but found them all very different. As a reuslt I decided to use the same information as their previous card but give it a completely new look with the idea of using opposites. Where they used a darker background I went instead for lighter backgrounds in my designs etc.
